Questions
Everything you're
probably wondering.
Do we need a developer or IT team to set this up?
Not at all. CampusChat is designed specifically for non-technical teams. You paste your URL, upload documents, and follow our setup wizard. No code, no APIs, no IT support required on your end.
What happens if the bot doesn't know the answer?
If a question falls outside your content, the bot politely says so and suggests the student contact your team directly. It never makes up answers or guesses — every response is grounded in what you've uploaded.
Can we use our existing WhatsApp number?
Yes — we connect to your existing number via the WhatsApp Business API. Students keep messaging the same number they already know. The only thing that changes is it now replies instantly, 24/7.
How does the chatbot stay updated when we change content?
Just re-upload a document or hit "Re-crawl" on your dashboard. The knowledge base updates immediately — no delay, no manual retraining, no support ticket needed.
Is student conversation data stored securely?
Yes. All conversations are encrypted, stored on secure servers, and never shared with third parties. You retain full ownership of your data. We comply with applicable data protection regulations.
Can a human take over a conversation if needed?
Yes. You can monitor live conversations from the dashboard and step in at any point. The bot can also be configured to escalate specific query types to a human agent automatically.
Does it work in regional languages?
Yes. The underlying LLM supports Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, and many other languages. If a student messages in Hindi, the bot responds in Hindi — naturally, without any configuration.
What's included in the onboarding?
Every paid plan includes a guided onboarding session where we help you crawl your site, upload key documents, and test the bot before going live. Growth and Enterprise plans include a dedicated onboarding call.
